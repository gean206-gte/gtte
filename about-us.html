About Us

Get together exporters is a fastest expanding B2B marketplace catering an all-in-one Marketing platform for all Micro to large business to promote their business with their products and services online. We promote our register buyers and sellers across the globe to start and expand their business with our end-to-end business solution. Our robust marketing solutions and tools are helping our authorized sellers and buyers to conduct international business in a transparent and cost-efficient way. Business-to-business or (B2B) is a transaction or business conducted between one business with another, such as a wholesaler and retailer. B2B transactions tend to happen in the supply chain as well where, one company will purchase raw materials from another to be used in the manufacturing process. Get together exporters aim to connect the world’s manufacturers directly with the wholesalers and retailers to make a successful transaction between them.
